About our Little Bakery


Jordan Jones

Jordan has suffered from a severe case of social anxiety since grade-school. Determined gluten was the source of his problems, he began a gluten-free diet. He still is socially awkward, but believes not eating gluten scientifically helps him cope. He is the founder of EJBakery, which he opened in January 2016.

Elijah Harry

Elijah Harry, nationally known investor and member of the "Triple Comma Club", stumbled accross Jordan during an anti-gluten protest while he was visiting his hometown. Jordan pitched Elijah on a gluten-free bakery/restaurant combination. With the help of Elijah's billion dollar bank account, EJBakery soon opened its doors.
